About Us

H2O is an open source predictive analytics platform. Unlike traditional analytics tools, H2O provides a combination of extraordinary math and high performance parallel processing with unrivaled ease of use. H2O speaks the language of data science with support for R, Python, Scala, Java and a robust REST API.

Event FAQs

What is the price of the ticket ?

Ticket price varies

Please refer to the registration section here for ticket options. Price will vary based on the days you want to attend and how early you purchase your ticket.

What is included in my ticket ?

The ticket price includes admission to the event on the days you have selected for your purchase. If you have purchased a ticket that includes Day 2 (November 10), your ticket includes admission to the after party and book signing events.

Are there hotel discounts ?

We have special rates at the Quality Inn and Suites at NASA Ames and the Crestview Hotel, both located in Mountain View. Click here for more information.

How do I get to the event? Will there be parking?

H2O World is held at the Computer History Museum in Mountain View. You can find the directions here. Free parking will be provided but may be limited for those who arrive late. The museum is located about 2 miles from the Mountain View Caltrain/VTA station.
