About Us

H2O is the leading open source machine learning platform for smarter applications. H2O.ai is the silicon valley software company supporting and developing H2O. Leading insurance, healthcare and financial services companies are using H2O to make smarter predictions about churn, pricing, fraud and more. H2O.ai is fostering a grassroots movement of systems engineers, data scientists, data developers and predictive analysts to move machine learning forward. A rapidly growing community of H2O users is now active in more than 5,000 companies worldwide. H2O.ai is a Gartner Cool Vendor in Data Science for 2015.

What is Our Goal?

H2O World is bringing together a community of H2O users, customers and experts to share their knowledge and know-how. Over the course of three days, you’ll learn how to build predictive models and run machine learning algorithms in Big Data Environments. You'll also learn how use machine learning to build intelligent applications. And finally, you will hear from featured production users of H2O with their specific use cases.

  • Hear from industry leaders Hilary Mason and Monica Rogati
  • Learn from the author of Elements of Statistical Learning, Dr. Rob Tibshirani, and Dr. Stephen Boyd, the author of Convex Optimization & Linear Matrix Inequalities in System and Control Theory
  • Participate in hands-on H2O data science training
  • See use cases from data scientists in industries such as ad tech, insurance, and finance, from companies like Progressive, Macy's, Nielsen Catalina Solutions, and AT&T.
